Coordinator & Billing FAQ
Can VennCare bill under Core Supports?
Yes. VennCare offers flexible billing for participants who have exhausted Capacity Building. VennCare bills under Core - Disability Related Health Supports using the following line items: 01_661_0128_1_3 (Assessment/Therapy - OT)
Does VennCare accept NDIA Managed participants?
Currently, VennCare accepts Plan-Managed and Self-Managed participants only. VennCare is in the progress of obtaining NDIS Registration for Agency Managed clients.
What Capacity Building codes does VennCare use?
For standard therapy and assessments (Improved Daily Living), VennCare uses: 15_617_0128_1_3 (Assessment/Therapy - General) 15_617_0118_1_3 (Assessment/Therapy - General)
Does VennCare accept Support at Home (Aged Care)?
Yes. VennCare provides Functional Assessments and Home Mods for Support at Home (SAP/ Former HCP) clients in South West Sydney.
How is travel billed?
VennCare follows the NDIS Pricing Arrangements (MMM1-3).
SWS Local (Edmondson Pk/Liverpool): Billed at actual travel time (typically minimal <30-40 mins).
Extended Zones: Capped at 40-60 mins depending on location.
